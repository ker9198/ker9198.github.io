Featured Speakers

>

Sam Altman

CEO of OpenAI

Sam Altman is a prominent figure in the technology sector, known for his role as co-founder and CEO of OpenAI, where he has led groundbreaking advancements in artificial intelligence, including the development of ChatGPT and DALL-E. Prior to OpenAI, he co-founded Loopt, a pioneering location-based social networking app, and served as president of Y Combinator from 2014 to 2019, mentoring numerous successful startups such as Airbnb and Reddit. His extensive experience encompasses entrepreneurship, investment in over 100 companies, and a commitment to ethical AI development.

>

Jensen Huang

Chief Executive and Co-Founder of Nvidia Corp.

Jensen Huang is an electrical engineer who co-founded NVIDIA in 1993, where he has served as CEO since its inception. He previously worked at Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) and LSI Logic, gaining valuable experience in semiconductor design and management. Under his leadership, NVIDIA pioneered the development of Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) and has significantly impacted the fields of gaming, artificial intelligence, and accelerated computing.
